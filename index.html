Claude Skills Visualizer

Explore your Claude Code skills in an interactive 3D space.

An interactive 3D web visualizer that auto-scans your local Claude Code skills directory and renders them as a force-directed graph. Each skill becomes a glowing orb you can click to inspect — revealing its description, trigger rules, and connections.

  • Auto-scans ~/.claude/skills for installed skills
  • Force-directed 3D graph layout with Three.js
  • Click any skill orb to see full details
  • Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom, sidebar navigation
  • Built entirely with AI-assisted workflows (Claude Code)

Solo project · macOS & iOS

AetherFocus

A music ambient focus app for deep work and study.

AetherFocus blends lo-fi ambience with fantasy worldbuilding to create immersive focus environments. Choose from themed atmospheric scenes with layered soundscapes, mix them live, and pair with a curated music station. Audio-reactive sphere visualizers pulse in sync with your music. A built-in Pomodoro timer, todo list, and multi-tab notes keep everything in one place.

  • 5 themed scenes with looping video backgrounds
  • Live ambience mixer — fire, rain, forest, wind
  • Audio-reactive sphere visualizers (mic or system audio)
  • Pomodoro timer, todo list, multi-tab notes
  • Premium sphere packs — one-time purchase, no subscription
  • macOS + iOS from a single Swift codebase
